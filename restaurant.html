The Restaurant & Bar

At Rosettes Boutique Hotel, Kampala, The Restaurant & Bar offers a refined culinary journey in an atmosphere of timeless elegance. Designed for comfort and sophistication, the space blends modern luxury with warm hospitality, creating the perfect setting for both intimate dining and relaxed social gatherings.

Our menu features a carefully curated selection of international and local flavors, crafted by skilled chefs using the finest ingredients. From signature dishes to expertly mixed cocktails, every detail is thoughtfully prepared to deliver an exceptional dining experience worthy of a boutique luxury hotel.

Hours

Breakfast: 7.00 am – 11.00 am (Daily)

Lunch: 12.00 noon – 2.00 pm (Daily)

Dinner: From 6.30 pm – Last order at 10.00 pm (Daily)

Dress Code

Smart casual attire is recommended to maintain an elegant dining atmosphere.

Terrace

The terrace is open for drinks only, offering a relaxed outdoor setting to enjoy premium beverages in the heart of Kampala.
