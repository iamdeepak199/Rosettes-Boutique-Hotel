Junior Suite

The Junior Suite at Rosettes Boutique Hotel is a refined sanctuary designed for guests who value comfort, privacy, and luxury. Thoughtfully styled with elegant interiors and modern finishes, the suite offers a serene escape in the heart of Kampala.

Every detail reflects our commitment to premium hospitality—from plush furnishings and soft lighting to a calm, spacious layout that allows you to relax, work, or unwind in absolute comfort. Ideal for both business and leisure travelers.

Check-in

  • Check-in from 9:00 AM – Anytime

  • Early check-in subject to availability

Check-out

  • Check-out before 12:00 noon

  • Express check-out available

Special Check-in Instructions

Guests will receive an email prior to arrival with detailed check-in information. Our front desk team will personally welcome you upon arrival. For any assistance, please contact the property using the details on your booking confirmation.

Pets

Pets are not permitted to ensure a serene environment for all guests.

Children and Extra Beds

Children are welcome. Complimentary stay applies when using existing bedding. Extra beds are available upon request at an additional charge.
